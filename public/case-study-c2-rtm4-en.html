C2 RTM-4 Device Monitoring Platform

Control room application for highway device monitoring. Led 4-person design team introducing hypothesis-driven design methodology and comprehensive design system for real-time dashboard customization.

🏢 Client: Movyon - Autostrade per l'Italia
👤 Role: Lead Product Designer (4 designers team)
📅 Duration: 4 months
🎯 Focus: Design System, Control Room UX

Design Process

01

Discovery & Hypothesis Formation

Introduced hypothesis-driven design methodology. Conducted stakeholder interviews with control room operators to identify pain points in device monitoring workflows and dashboard customization needs.

02

Design System Foundation

Created comprehensive design system with reusable components, established visual language, and defined interaction patterns for consistent user experience across all monitoring interfaces.

03

Team Leadership & Coordination

Led 4-person design team through collaborative design sprints, established design review processes, and mentored junior designers on hypothesis testing and validation methods.

04

Real-time Dashboard Design

Designed customizable dashboard interfaces for real-time device monitoring, optimized for videowall displays, with intuitive data visualization and alert management systems.

05

Testing & Iteration

Conducted usability testing with control room operators, validated design hypotheses with quantitative metrics, and iterated based on operational feedback for optimal performance.

Key Innovations

🧪

Hypothesis-Driven Design

Pioneered data-driven design approach with testable hypotheses, validation metrics, and iterative improvement cycles for evidence-based decision making.

🎨

Company-wide Design System

Established scalable design system adopted across entire organization, ensuring consistency and accelerating development of new monitoring features.

📊

Videowall Optimization

Designed specialized interfaces for multi-screen videowall environments with optimized information hierarchy and visual clarity for 24/7 operations.

⚡

Real-time Data Visualization

Created intuitive dashboard components for monitoring thousands of highway devices simultaneously with instant alert systems and status indicators.
