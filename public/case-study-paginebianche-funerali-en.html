Key Innovations

Solutions designed for a sensitive experience

Multi-Filter Search

Advanced search system to find services by location, type and budget with relevant results.

Guided Quote

Step-by-step wizard to request quotes easily, guiding users through their choices.

Online Obituaries

Dedicated section to publish and share obituaries with digital condolence messages.

Dedicated portal for funeral operators to manage profile, quotes and platform visibility.
