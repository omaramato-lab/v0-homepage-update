How It Works

Step 1: Briefing

I understand your product, users and challenges. We define scope and flows to analyze.

Step 2: Qualitative User Testing

I personally navigate the product following user scenarios. I identify usability issues.

Step 3: Analysis & Recommendations

I analyze problem patterns. Create recommendations based on UX best practices and principles.

Step 4: Redesign

Create high-quality mockups for critical screens, implementing proposed solutions.

Step 5: Delivery & Walkthrough

Deliver complete report and present findings in 60-minute video session.
