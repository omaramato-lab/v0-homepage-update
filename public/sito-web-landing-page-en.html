FAQ

How long does it take to complete a site?
Typically 4-8 weeks of design, depending on complexity. Discovery one week, design 2-3 weeks, prototyping and reviews 1-2 weeks.
Do you include development?
No, I provide design, prototyping and developer hand-off. I can recommend reliable developers and support during implementation.
What happens after launch?
30 days of post-launch support included for fixes and minor adjustments. Monthly support packages available for ongoing maintenance.
Do you do updates to existing sites?
Yes, I provide audits and redesigns of existing sites. We analyze performance, identify UX issues and create improvement strategy.
Can I make changes during design?
Absolutely, it's part of the process. I include 2-3 design review rounds where we gather feedback and iterate together.
